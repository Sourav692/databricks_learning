The two foundations

Almost everything in Databricks data engineering rests on these. Learn the stages on top of them.

🪨

Delta Lake

The storage format that makes data reliable — like a spreadsheet that never corrupts, remembers its history, and supports updates & deletes.

🛡️

Unity Catalog

The governance layer that controls who can see and do what — across every table, file, and AI asset, using catalog.schema.table.

A note on naming

In June 2025 Databricks grouped its data engineering tools under one umbrella called Lakeflow. Old names still appear in many docs — here's the translation:

Delta Live Tables (DLT)  →  Lakeflow Spark Declarative Pipelines (SDP)

Workflows  →  Lakeflow Jobs

(managed connectors)  →  Lakeflow Connect

(new)  →  Lakeflow Designer — visual, no-code / natural-language pipeline builder

Existing DLT pipelines keep working unchanged — it's mostly a rebrand plus new capabilities. Lakeflow (Connect · SDP · Designer · Jobs) is the umbrella for end-to-end data engineering.
